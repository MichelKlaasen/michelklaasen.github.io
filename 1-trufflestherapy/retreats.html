Discover Yourself

Our retreats are all about you. We are here to support and guide you through the deeply transformative experience magic truffles have to offer you. It is our aim to provide you with the best and safest set & setting for your journey.

Our Holistic Approach

In our experiences both as guides and journeyers, the psilocybin fungi are teachers and healers. It is our honor to create and provide a comfortable space for you to experience the healing powers of psilocybin truffles. These substances contain nature’s healing intelligence and open your consciousness up to the same force lying within your own physical and emotional body.
